After switching majors and completing my first year in Electrical Engineering, I received my first summer internship at Jet Propulsion Laboratories (JPL).

There I supported Flight Hardware Logistics Program (FHLP) in hardware captures, inventory registration, delivery and inventory transactions. I also assisted in the operation of General Inventory, supporting customers and receiving material, and inventory transactions.



Jet Propulsion Laboratories is well renowned for the construction and operation of planetary robotic spacecraft, such as the Mars Science Laboratory (MSL).
